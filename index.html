My Story


After nearly six years of studying literature and theory in college and grad school, I discovered coding in February 2015 and decided to change what I was doing with my life.

I left my PhD program and began to devote all of my waking time to studying software and web development. I self-studied Python for 4 months (August - November 2015) and then received a full scholarship to attend Coding House-- a fully-immersive JavaScript bootcamp in Fremont, California. In April, I began working as an apprentice developer at Cyrus Innovation, a software consultancy in New York City.
